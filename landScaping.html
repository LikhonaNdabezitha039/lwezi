LANDSCAPING

What is Landscaping?

Landscaping is the art and process of modifying the visible features of an area of land. It goes beyond simple gardening to encompass the holistic design, planning, and implementation of both softscape (living elements) and hardscape (non-living elements) to create an aesthetically pleasing and functional outdoor environment.

Principles of Landscape Design
  1. Unity: The landscape should feel cohesive, with elements that work together through repetition, consistency, and a unified style.
  2. Balance: Creating visual equilibrium, which can be symmetrical (formal) or asymmetrical (informal).
  3. Proportion & Scale: Ensuring the size of elements fits comfortably with each other and with the surrounding buildings.
