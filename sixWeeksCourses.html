SUMMARY OF SIX-WEEK COURSES

Our six-week programs are short, hands-on, and designed to provide immediate, practical skills for community employment, home management, and personal growth.

Child Minding

This course trains learners in professional child care and early childhood development. Participants learn how to ensure children’s safety, plan age-appropriate activities, manage daily routines, and provide emotional and educational support. Ideal for those seeking work as child minders, au pairs, or early years assistants.

Learn More

Cooking

A practical cooking course that builds essential culinary and kitchen management skills. Students learn safe food handling, meal preparation, nutrition basics, menu planning, and presentation. Perfect for home cooks or those starting a catering or hospitality career.

Learn More

Garden Maintenance

This course focuses on the practical aspects of maintaining gardens and green spaces. Topics include soil care, weeding, pruning, lawn mowing, composting, and basic irrigation. Students gain knowledge to keep outdoor environments healthy and visually appealing, supporting both residential and commercial landscapes.
