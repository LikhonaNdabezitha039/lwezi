What is Sewing?

Sewing is the craft of fastening or attaching objects using stitches made with a needle and thread. It's one of the oldest textile arts, used for everything from creating garments and home decor to repairs and artistic expression.

Types of Sewing
  1. Hand Sewing
  2. Machine Sewing
  3. Embroidery
  4. Quilting
