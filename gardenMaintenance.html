GARDEN MAINTENANCE

What is Garden Maintenance?

Garden maintaining is the ongoing process of nurturing and caring for your garden to ensure its health, productivity, and beauty. It involves a series of regular tasks that change with the seasons, all aimed at supporting plant growth and managing the garden ecosystem.

Why is Garden Maintenance Important?
  1. Promotes Plant Health: Regular care prevents diseases, deters pests, and encourages strong growth.
  2. Maximizes Yield & Blooms: Proper maintenance leads to more flowers, fruits, and vegetables.
  3. Saves Money: A well-maintained garden reduces the need to replace plants and correct big problems later.
