SUMMARY OF SIX-MONTH COURSES

Explore our six-month professional training programs designed to build practical skills, self-reliance, and employability within local communities.

First Aid

This course provides essential emergency response training. Participants learn CPR, how to use an AED, treat wounds and fractures, manage choking, and stabilize injuries before professional help arrives. Gain confidence in saving lives in any situation.

Learn More

Sewing

Learn the fundamentals of both hand and machine sewing. Students gain skills in garment construction, pattern cutting, alterations, hemming, and creative design. This course encourages independence and income generation through clothing repair and tailoring.

Learn More

Landscaping

Develop expertise in outdoor design and garden management. Learn about plant selection, irrigation systems, soil preparation, and sustainable maintenance. This course empowers students to create beautiful, eco-friendly gardens and functional green spaces.

Learn More

Life Skills

This course focuses on personal and professional development. Topics include financial literacy, time management, stress control, effective communication, and confidence-building. Graduates are equipped with vital competencies for success in both work and life.
